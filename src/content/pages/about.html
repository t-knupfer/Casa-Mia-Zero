About Domus Zero

We focus on the sustainable energy transition for private homes in Italy. We transform private homes to no longer rely on fossil fuels.

In doing so, we minimize the reliance on fossil fuels while significantly reducing energy costs.

We have experience from hundreds of successfully installed sustainability projects. We have analyzed them carefully to optimize the solar and heat pump installation process to the benefit of our clients.
