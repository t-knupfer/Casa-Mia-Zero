Our All-Inclusive Service

Domus Zero offers an all-inclusive turnkey solution:

  • Proactive collaboration with your Comune and your local electricity provider to propose viable sustainable solutions for your home
  • Integrated installations of solar and heat pump systems based on fixed price offering within a few weeks from initial discusssion
  • Use of high quality, efficient, and reliable equipment/systems.
  • Realization of attractive return on investment, reduced energy and heating costs, and minimized CO2 emissions.
  • Carefree transition including concept development, project implementation management, disposal of old systems as well as all administrative processes with the Comune, the energy provider, etc.
  • Minimized disruption for our customers during the implementation
  • Optional: Regular services for the installed system
