Our Process

Our Process

Turn Your Home Sustainable in Mere Weeks

  • 01

    Contact Us

    Via phone, email, or website and we will get back to you in no time. We will also ask you to prepare some basic data/information/etc. E.g. Last monthly/annually energy bill, exact address, some specific picture of house and existing energy systems, etc.

  • 02

    Schedule Initial Zoom/MSTeam Call

    We will ask you to share the information we requested under point 01.

  • 03

    Remote Feasibility Analysis

    We will do remote feasibility analyses including location, Comune, space, ... of your property.

  • 04

    Tailored solution

    We will schedule with you a 3h site visits to collect all required data (ideally while you are present).

  • 05

    High Quality Installation

    High quality installation of integrated solar panel and heat pump solution all with minimum disruption to your home.

  • 06

    Tailored Solution

    We develop a tailored and detailed solution including a specific offer. If you accept the offer, we will inform you within a few days about the detailed implementation plan/schedule.

  • 07

    Hand Over

    Final turnkey hand over of property-specific sustainable solution with minimized usage of fossil fuels.

  • 08

    Support

    Post-realization support as needed and requested by you.
